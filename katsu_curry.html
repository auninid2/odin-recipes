Katsu Curry

Katsu curry is a popular Japanese dish that consists of a breaded, deep-fried cutlet (in my case chuicken) served with a pureed curry sauce and rice. It is a simple and comforting meal that is enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  1. Onions
  2. Chicken breast
  3. A medium sized apple
  4. Tomato paste
  5. Curry powder
  6. Garam masala
  7. Meat broth
  8. Flour
  9. Eggs
  10. Panko breadcrumbs
  11. Rice

Steps

  • Prepare the curry sauce by sauteeing onions, apple, and tomato paste in a pot.
  • Add curry powder and garam masala to the pot and cook until fragrant.
  • Pour in the meat broth and let the sauce simmer until thickened.
  • Season with salt and sugar to taste.
  • Prepare the chicken by seasoning with salt and pepper, then coating in flour, egg, and panko breadcrumbs.
  • Fry the chicken in oil until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Serve the chicken with rice and curry sauce.
