Dolma

Dolma is a popular Middle Eastern dish that consists of grape leaves stuffed with a mixture of rice, pine nuts, and spices.

Ingredients

  1. Grape leaves
  2. Rice
  3. Pine nuts
  4. Onions
  5. Tomato paste
  6. Lemon juice
  7. Spices (such as mint, parsley, and dill)

Steps

  • Prepare the grape leaves by blanching them in boiling water and removing the stems.
  • Prepare the stuffing by cooking rice, pine nuts, onions, tomato paste, lemon juice, and spices together.
  • Place a spoonful of the stuffing in the center of each grape leaf and fold the sides over to form a roll.
  • Arrange the dolma in a pot, seam side down, and cover with water.
  • Simmer the dolma until the rice is cooked and the grape leaves are tender.
  • Serve the dolma with yogurt or a squeeze of lemon juice.
